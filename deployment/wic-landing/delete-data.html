What Happens When You Delete

Your account and all personal data are permanently deleted from our servers. Community contributions (product availability reports) are anonymized so they can continue helping other families. This action cannot be undone.

Option 1: Delete From the App

The fastest way to delete your data is directly in the app:

  1. Open Settings Tap the gear icon on the home screen
  2. Go to Privacy Settings → Privacy
  3. Tap "Delete My Account" At the bottom of the Privacy screen
  4. Confirm deletion Type the confirmation phrase when prompted. Your data is deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request by Email

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to make your request by email:

Send a Deletion Request

Email us at wic.benefits.app@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Data".

Include any details that help us locate your account (e.g., the device you used the app on). Since the app uses anonymous device IDs rather than email or phone login, we may ask you to verify your identity through the app if possible.

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm deletion by email.

What Gets Deleted

The following data is permanently removed from our servers when you delete your account:

Data Action
Account & device identifier Deleted Immediate
Household members & participants Deleted Immediate
Benefit balances & manual entries Deleted Immediate
Shopping carts & cart items Deleted Immediate
Transaction & checkout history Deleted Immediate
Formula alerts & subscriptions Deleted Immediate
Push notification tokens & settings Deleted Immediate
Notification history Deleted Immediate
Product availability reports Anonymized — your identity is removed but the report stays to help other families
On-device data (benefits, settings) Cleared Immediate

Data We Never Store

The following data is never retained on our servers, so there is nothing to delete:

  • Location data (used only during search, not stored)
  • Photos from barcode or benefit statement scanning
  • Your real name, email, phone, SSN, or WIC card number

After Deletion

Once your data is deleted:

  • You can continue using the barcode scanner without an account
  • A new anonymous account is created automatically if you set up a household again
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered
